How to Play

1
Sort by Color
Move each bead from the bottom tray to its matching color zone using the magnetic wand.
2
Match the Animal
Guide beads to the correct animal — Dolphin gets blue, Crab gets red, Turtle gets green.
3
Count 1 to 10
Place beads one by one — counting aloud as you go. Fill each numbered zone to complete the challenge!
